🍪 Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The short version: We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party analytics on this website. The only thing stored in your browser is a small note confirming you dismissed our cookie banner, and a temporary session token that keeps your Amy chat conversation running while you are on this page. Both disappear when you close your browser or clear your data.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device when you visit them. They are used for a variety of purposes — from keeping you logged in, to tracking your behaviour across websites for advertising. This policy explains what we store, why, and for how long.

2. What cookies does TradesFlow AI use?

We use the absolute minimum necessary to make this website function. We do not use any advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or analytics platforms such as Google Analytics.

Name Type Purpose Duration
cookieOk localStorage token Remembers that you have dismissed our cookie notice so we don't show it again on repeat visits. Persistent (until you clear browser data)
Amy chat session In-memory (JavaScript) Holds your current Amy chat conversation in your browser's memory so it continues correctly during your visit. Nothing is written to disk or sent to any third-party service. The conversation is relayed to our Cloudflare Worker to generate Amy's replies and is not stored after the session ends. Session only — deleted when you close the tab

3. Third-party services

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may set its own cookies or collect minimal technical data (such as your IP address) as part of this request. We have no control over Google's cookie behaviour. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

If you book a call via the Calendly link on this site, you will be taken to Calendly's website, which operates under its own cookie and privacy policy. We do not embed Calendly on this page in a way that would cause it to load cookies without your interaction.

4. What we do NOT do

  • We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party analytics or advertising tracking service.
  • We do not sell, share, or trade your browsing data with any third party.
  • We do not use cookies to build a profile of your interests or browsing behaviour.
  • We do not use retargeting cookies.

5. Your rights and how to control cookies

You have the right to control what is stored in your browser at all times. You can clear cookies and localStorage data at any time through your browser settings. Doing so will not affect the functionality of this website — you may simply see the cookie notice again on your next visit.

All major browsers allow you to block cookies entirely or on a per-site basis. The links below explain how to manage cookies in common browsers:
