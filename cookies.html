The Process

What happens when a customer gets in touch

1

Customer gets in touch via your website

Whether they land on your website at 2pm or 2am — on a Sunday or a bank holiday — Amy is there immediately. No waiting, no voicemail, no missed opportunity.

2

Amy holds a natural conversation and collects the details

Amy introduces herself as an AI receptionist and guides the customer through the conversation naturally. She collects everything you need: name, phone number, full address, job description, and urgency — all automatically. She speaks in plain, conversational English — not a rigid script.

3

You get notified instantly

The moment the conversation ends, the full lead lands in your Google Sheet and you receive a WhatsApp notification (or email if you prefer) with all the details — even while you're flat out on a job.

4

You call back and win the job

You have everything you need before you even pick up the phone. The customer already feels looked after — and you get to call back with the full context of their enquiry, ready to convert.

5

Amy keeps working after the job is done

On Professional and Growth plans, Amy doesn't stop there. She follows up on quotes that go quiet, chases overdue invoices at day 3, 7 and 14, and sends a personalised review request after every completed job — all automatically.
