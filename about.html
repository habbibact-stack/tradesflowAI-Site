Our Story

Built for the real world of trades

The reality for most plumbers, electricians, builders and heating engineers is the same: you're on a job, your phone rings, you can't answer, and that customer moves on to the next number on Google. You never even knew they called.

We built Amy to fix that. She holds a real conversation, captures what matters — name, number, full address, job description and urgency — and gets the details to you immediately so you can call back and win the work.

But we didn't stop at the receptionist. We looked at all the other places trades businesses leak money: quotes that go cold because nobody follows up, invoices that sit unpaid for weeks because chasing feels awkward, and happy customers who never leave a review because nobody asked them at the right moment.

TradesFlow AI addresses all of it, automatically, in the background, while you get on with the job you actually love doing.

We handle everything from setup to ongoing support. You don't need to understand AI, you don't need a web developer, and you don't need to change how you work. TradesFlow AI slots in around you — live within 48 hours, with zero technical knowledge required on your part.
